About me

I am a Ph.D. student co-trained at the Dornsife Spatial Sciences Institute, Department of Sociology & Keck Department of Population and Public Health Sciences at the University of Southern California. Advised by Dr. John P. Wilson and Dr. Megan M. Herting, my research focuses on how physical and cultural environment interact and influence mental health. My research interests sit at the intersection of Health Geography, Environmental Perception, Mental Health, and GeoAI Applications.
